FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers! Find everything you need to know about ordering, delivery, payments, and more below. Explore the common asked by our clients

What is MyFarmProduct?

MyFarmProduct is an online marketplace that connects you directly with local farmers and producers, delivering fresh, high-quality products to your doorstep.

How does MyFarmProduct work?

Browse our platform, select the farm products you want, place your order, and enjoy quick delivery straight from the source.

How do I place an order?

Simply create an account, browse the marketplace, add products to your cart, and proceed to checkout.

Is there a minimum order requirement?

No, you can order as little or as much as you like. However, minimum order amounts may apply for free delivery promotions.

What are the delivery options?

We offer standard, express, and scheduled deliveries. Delivery availability may vary depending on your location.

Can I track my order?

Yes! Once your order is placed, you’ll receive tracking information via email or SMS.

How do I become a seller on MyFarmProduct?

Click on the “Become a Seller” link on our homepage and fill out the registration form. Our team will guide you through the onboarding process.
